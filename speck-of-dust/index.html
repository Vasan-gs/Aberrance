Speck of Dust

Leave a Comment / Life is Beautiful / By Vasan
  •  
  •  
  •  
  •  
  •  

Silly beings like a speck of dust in the grand multiverse

From the heights of skies
Life seems so small in size

When its future of gold you seek
Precious life around you turns bleak

So, live for the little things
And everything life brings

Live for the sheer heck of it
Life’s short for bad vibes and shit

Own the good and bad in your story
Blaming the rest won’t glow your glory

Out of 1000s of words, thus I share with you 3
And if something’s meant to be, just let it be

God shared the wisdom and love he had. So, aren’t we supposed to share what’s not ours in the first place?

Vasan
